    • calculate_v3_master_key(inquiry_number: bigint): bigint

      Calculate the master key for the parental control using the v3 algorithm. The inquire number cannot be bigger than 10 digits.

      Remember that the given master key must be presented with the correct amount of leading zeroes to always have 8 digits.

      Only works on Switch (from 1.0.0 to 7.0.1).
